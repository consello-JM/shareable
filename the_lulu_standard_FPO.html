01

the belief

why product is first

lululemon did not lose what made it great. It drifted from it. The work is reconnection: to the product obsession that created a category, the community that gave it soul, and the standard of excellence that made it the reference point for an industry. Product leadership is not a marketing strategy. It is the business strategy.

Product leads.

The source of credibility, the reason guests build their lives around us, and the only proof the market cannot argue with.

Retail amplifies.

The store is not a point of sale. It is a point of belief, where the product is felt and the community is real.

Marketing connects.

Not louder. Sharper. We set the agenda for what performance lifestyle means. We do not chase it.

Mission and Vision are anchored to lululemon's existing official language. Consumer and Position are proposed for Return to Form. Tap each to expand.

The guest

Women and men who build their lives around movement and wellbeing. Discerning, devoted, and done with average.

No standalone consumer statement exists in lululemon's official language today. This is new articulation, written to span both the core women's guest and the fast-growing men's business, not one gender. They reward premium product, real human service, and experiences that connect. Draft for review.

06

what we hold

commitments and refusals

A standard is clearest stated both ways: what we always do, and what we never do. These are principle-level convictions. The granular execution rules live in the brand guide, governed by this Standard.

We alwaysWe never
Lead with product, and earn premium through it.
Discount our way to demand.
Sense culture early and set the trend.
Chase a trend late. The wide-leg miss does not repeat.
Choose ambassadors for expertise and credibility.
Choose them for follower count.
Test fit and fabric at the center of creation. Zero defect is the floor.
Ship a product we have not stood behind. The recall does not repeat.
Make every store a point of belief, online and in person.
Build a store that feels like everyone else's.
Edit the line. Fewer, better, deeper.
Add what we should perfect by subtracting.

Recommendation: keep these principle-level pairs in the Standard. The detailed do and do-not rules (promotional cadence, imagery specifications, ambassador tiers) belong in the brand guide.

the lululemon flywheel

how the standard compounds

Held consistently, the Standard is self-reinforcing. Product is the first push, and every turn makes the next one easier. This is why product-first is an engine, not a slogan.

Superior product earns guest trust, which fuels community, which creates authentic brand heat, which supports premium pricing power, which funds reinvestment back into product. Break any link and the wheel slows. Hold the Standard and it accelerates.
