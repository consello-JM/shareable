One place where Plainview can learn, build, and grow — together.

Why this, why now

A 70-year-old building, ready for its best chapter yet

This isn't starting from scratch. It's taking a building Plainview already loves and giving it a future that serves more of us, more of the time.

The moment

Act now, on our terms

The building needs investment to keep serving the community. This is the chance to shape that future deliberately, rather than react to it later.

The model

No new burden on taxpayers

The vision is built so a mission-aligned operator finances and runs the campus while the district keeps ownership. The community gains; the tax bill doesn't.

The payoff

Something for every household

Young children, students, and working parents alike. Few investments touch this many families at once.
